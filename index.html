Welcome to THE LOOK STUDIO, your premier destination for exceptional tattoo and piercing services. Located in the heart of KOCHI , we pride ourselves on offering a clean, professional, and comfortable environment where your artistic vision can come to life. At LOOK STUDIO we understand that getting a tattoo or piercing is a personal and meaningful experience. Our team of highly skilled and experienced artists is dedicated to providing you with top-notch artistry, exceptional customer service, and a commitment to your safety and satisfaction.

ARTISTS

Scott Campbell

Founder, Tattoo Artist.
Guinness Record Holder.

Scott Campbell is a New York-based American artist and tattoo artist whose clients include Howard Stern, Sting, Robert Downey Jr., Courtney Love, Orlando Bloom, Josh Hartnett, and Marc Jacobs.

Brian Keith Thompson

Co-Founder, Piercing Artist

Brian Keith Thompson is the proprietor and Chief Piercing Officer of The World Famous Body Electric Tattoo, a Hollywood landmark situated on Melrose Avenue that stands as a Los Angeles institution and must-see destination for international travelers.

Mira Mariah

Famous Tattoo Artist.

Mira Mariah’s beautiful line drawn tattoos are immediately recognisable. They are what she would describe as femme – from the delicate designs.They almost look more like fashion illustrations – which makes sense given that Mariah’s professional background and first love is fashion.

Anthony Michaels

Tattoo Artist.

Anthony Michaels is a professional creative. Anthony has had a phenomenal talent for creating art since a kid. He grew up excelling at sports like football, track and field, and most of all, basketball, for which he attended Pima Community College. After finishing college and pursuing his professional basketball career, he got into tattooing. As a teenager, he drew pictures for the walls of local tattoo shops. So it seemed inevitable for the self-taught artist to start tattooing.
